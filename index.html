Bocchi The Rock

Bocchi the Rock! is a slice-of-life anime series based on the manga by Aki Hamaji. The story follows Hitori "Bocchi" Gotou, a socially anxious high school girl who dreams of becoming a rock star. Despite her talent for playing guitar, Bocchi struggles to make friends and often feels isolated.
